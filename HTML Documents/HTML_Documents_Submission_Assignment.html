Introduction to Sharks

From large to small, from agressive to docile, nature has provided a plethora of shark species! Most people view sharks as nothing but aquatic deathmachines, but there was only 57 worldwide and unprovoked bites in 2022. The media has also played a part in instilling fear of sharks within us. A variety of sharks have been featured in cinema, from cult classics like JAWS to newer realeases like The Meg. Of course no one could forget Sharknado either! JAWS in partcular has an immediately recognizable musical arrangement revolving around two notes, as well as one of the top memorable lines in cinematic history:

"You're gonna need a bigger boat." -Roy Schreider
